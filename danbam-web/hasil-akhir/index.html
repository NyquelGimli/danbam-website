Our DanBam

DanBam is a Korean restaurant that offers many varieties of traditional Korean food that we serve with a modern touch. We first started the company at 57-28 Itaewon-dong, Yongsan-gu, Seoul. The company has grown to be one of the best restaurants for traditional Korean food in Korea, having a several branches all over Korea.

History

Itaewon Class (이태원 클라 쓰) is a series of South Korean television in 2020, starring by Park Seo-joon, Kim Da-mi, Yoo Jae-myung and Kwon Nara. The series is based on the webtoon of the same name and is the first series produced by the film distribution company Showbox.
