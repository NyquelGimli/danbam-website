History

Itaewon Class (이태원 클라 쓰) is a series of South Korean television in 2020, starring by Park Seo-joon, Kim Da-mi, Yoo Jae-myung and Kwon Nara. The series is based on the webtoon of the same name and is the first series produced by the film distribution company Showbox.
